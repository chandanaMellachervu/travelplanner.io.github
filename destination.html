Destinations

GOA

Goa is a state in western India with coastlines stretching along the Arabian Sea.



TAJ MAHAL

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the south bank of the Yamuna river in the Indian city of Agra.



TIRUPATHI

Tirupati is known as the abode of the Hindu god Venkateshvara, Lord of Seven Hills.



INDIA GATE

India Gate is one of many British monuments built by order of the Imperial War Graves Commission (later renamed Commonwealth War Graves Commission).



SHANIWAR WADA

Shaniwar Wada is a historical fortification in the city of Pune, India.



VARNASI

The land of Varanasi (Kashi) has been the ultimate pilgrimage spot for Hindus for ages.



OOTY

Ooty is a gorgeous hill station in Tamil Nadu, and is considered a haven for nature lovers from all the country.



MANALI

Manali is synonymous streams and birdsong, forests and orchards and grandees of snow-capped mountains.



GOLDEN TEMPLE

The Golden temple is famous for its full golden dome, it is one of the most sacred pilgrim spots for Sikhs.
